So... why classless CSS?

  • Less complexity, more semantics
  • Leverage browser defaults
    instead of override their behavior
  • Accessibility and performance
    out of the box

What is Classless CSS?

  • No classes, just pure HTML elements
  • Style is inferred from semantics
  • HTML stays clean, accessible, and fast

The New Minimalist Movement

  • Classless frameworks (Pico.css, Water.css)
  • Respect and enhance native browser styles
  • Fewer overrides, more semantics
  • Accessibility and responsiveness built-in

Popular Classless Frameworks

  • Pico.css – beautiful defaults & theming
  • Water.css – zero-config elegance
  • Almond.css – opinionated and light

Why Pico.css?

  • Responsive, minimal and beautiful
  • Works out of the box with semantic tags
  • Perfect for clean prototyping
